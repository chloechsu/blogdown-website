For Women

  • Women in Data Science: A series of events held at 20+ locations worldwide.
  • IAS Women and Mathematics Program: A 2-week conference for women in math at all levels. A great mix of talks, mini courses, and volunteering activities. Plus, you get to run a 5k!
  • Graduate Research Opportunities for Women (GROW): A 2-day conference for undergraduate women interested in graduate math research.

General

  • Mathematical Foundations of Computer Science: A European conference focused on theoretical computer science.
  • Probability and Statistics in Forensic Science: This is a one-time program, but there are many other workshops and programs at Issac Newton Institute.
  • Pacific Coast Undergraduate Math Conference: A friendly undergrad math conference, nice experience as a first conference.
